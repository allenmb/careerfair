Food

To find the best places to get a lunch or dinner

  • Browse through events on Handshake. Look for information sessions and workshops around 11-1 and 5-7. Any later and you'll only get dessert.
  • Ask students which companies bring the best food. You're going to want to look for big names in engineering fields. In the author's experience, the business major information sessions get way too many people for them to bring a meal for everyone.
  • Keep an eye open as you walk between classes. Lots of University-sponsored events are advertised around campus.
  • A good list of events for the Spring STEM Fair can be found in the Events section of this website.

Resources

Click the icons on the left to find more resources that will get you free food and swag

Handshake is BYU's new career center service. Despite its shortcomings, it has an easily searchable list of events for finding meals.

Go to BYU's clubs website to find BYU chapters of professional associations. Around career fair times, these clubs host events for students to interact with recruiters and there's usually food involved.
